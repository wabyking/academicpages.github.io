Benyou Wang

Mail:   wabyking@gmail.com

Currently, I am a second-year Ph.D. student, as well as a Marie Curie Researcher at University of Padova, Italy. Beyond industrial backgournd (one-year full-time work in Tencent, Shenzhen focuing on NLP and Recommendation), I am aiming to develop a novel mathematically-inspired formalism/models/inovations for language understanding, which can also be applicable to high-level applications. In relatively-short academic experience, one of my co-authored papers won the SIGIR best paper honorable mention reward in 2017, and another paper  with sharing first-authorship received NAACL 2019 Best Explainable paper.
